Our story is amazing

we were born in 2008

    Foundress: Diana M.

    The brand arrived in the country in 2008: today it has more than 130 stores where more than 1,700 partners work. From its first store in Alto Palermo, it sought to surprise its customers with a unique experience. Today the Di Coffee Experience is enjoyed in different formats and locations around the country. Within the framework of its 10 years, it launched the Mobile app of its loyalty program, Di Coffee Rewards, which today has more than 130,000 members.

    Inspiration

    Inspire and nurture the human spirit: One person, one cup and one community at a time. These are the principles that govern the fulfillment of our mission day by day: Each store is part of a community and we take our responsibility as good neighbors seriously. We want to be welcome wherever we do business. We are able to contribute positively because we work to achieve a union between partners, customers and the community to cooperate every day. Now we realize that the dimension of our responsibility and that our potential to do good is even greater than before. Once again, the world looks to Di Coffee to set the new standard. We will be leaders.

    We fulfill this mission with the commitment to:

    It is our commitment to conduct business in a way that contributes positively to everyone and to our planet, from the way we buy coffee and reduce the impact on our environment, to the way we engage with our communities. It is doing things as we have always done them and it is using all our strength to do it well. And thanks to being with us, you are also part of Di Coffee Shared Planet. More than coffee, passion to improve the world.
